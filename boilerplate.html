Hello, everyone my name is Carlissa and I'm from Georgia. This is a list of my favorite things:

    Thankfully, I can find all of my interests here in the Columbia community. For dance, the Barnard Dance department is very accessible to non-majors. I got a mild knee injury last semester so I couldn't continue with ballet this year. But I was able to take pilates this semester which so far has been really enjoyable and helpful.

  2. Knitting


    As for knitting, I'm in the club "Gosh Yarn It" which is a student organization for knitters and crocheters. Though I haven't been to many meetings, the people there are very interesting and I'm looking forward to hanging out with them more.

  3. Books


    In respect to books, I'm probably taking one too many English classes but all in all I'm very happy with the texts we're discussing. ALso I love going to used bookstores like the Strand and whenever I finally dig myself out of the pile of homework I'm in, I have a nice little niche of books on my desk awaiting me.
